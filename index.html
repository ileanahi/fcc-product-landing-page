Pricing

Basic

Free

  • Limited to TWO dives in pursuit until you earn enough rockets to unlock more
  • Redeem rockets for certifications or to pursue more dives OR Pay $2 per dive and $2 per dive certification

Unlimited Dives

$10/year

  • Immediate access to pursue as many dives as you want
  • Dive certification attempts remain $2 each or certifiable via rockets

All Access

$35/year

  • Immediate access to pursue as many dives as you want
  • Apply for certifications as soon as you are eligible
  • Gain special guidance and attention from our staff

Your membership fee is considered tax-deductible as a charitable donation within the USA.
